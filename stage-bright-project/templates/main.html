Our Mission

Public speaking is the number one fear in America. People are more
afraid of speaking in front of a crowd than they are afraid of heights,
spiders, and even death. Our goal is to change that.

We want to empower aspiring public speakers with the confidence to
speak about whatever they are most passionate about. Whether it's dinosaurs,
sports, or saving the world, we give you the tools to prepare and practice your
message. To get started, check out some of our helpful resources, or practice
your speech using our teleprompter and our voice recorder!
